Numerical Modelling

Employing advanced numerical modeling software, Pejuni creates highly detailed and realistic simulations of mining scenarios. We develop three-dimensional linear and non-linear models that capture the intricacies of shallow, deep, or high extraction ratio mines. Our models facilitate accurate predictions, allowing for improved mine planning and performance optimization..

Our Numerical Modelling service at Pejuni involves the use of advanced computational techniques and software to simulate and analyze complex geotechnical and mining scenarios. It enables us to predict the behavior of soil, rock, and structures under various conditions, providing valuable insights for design, risk assessment, and decision-making. Here's a closer look at our Numerical Modelling service:

Model Development:

We begin by developing numerical models that represent the geotechnical and mining systems of interest. These models are based on mathematical equations and computational algorithms that simulate the physical behavior of the materials and structures involved. We consider factors such as soil properties, rock mechanics, groundwater flow, stress distribution, and boundary conditions to accurately capture the real-world behavior of the system.

Geotechnical Applications:

Numerical modelling allows us to address a wide range of geotechnical challenges. We can simulate and analyze slope stability, soil and rock deformation, foundation behavior, soil-structure interaction, groundwater seepage, and other geotechnical phenomena. By incorporating site-specific data and engineering parameters, we can make informed predictions and optimize designs to ensure the stability, safety, and efficiency of geotechnical projects.

Mining Applications:

Numerical modelling is also instrumental in addressing mining-related challenges. We can simulate and analyze open pit stability, underground excavation methods, rock fragmentation, subsidence, stress redistribution, and mine ventilation systems. By assessing these factors, we can optimize mine designs, develop appropriate support systems, and mitigate risks associated with mining operations.


N Numerical Modelling

Risk Assessment: Numerical modelling plays a crucial role in assessing and managing risks in geotechnical and mining projects. By simulating different scenarios and analyzing the response of the system, we can identify potential failure mechanisms, assess their likelihood, and evaluate their consequences. This information allows us to develop effective risk mitigation strategies and optimize project safety..
Design Optimization: Numerical modelling enables us to optimize designs by evaluating various alternatives and analyzing their performance. By simulating different design configurations and parameters, we can assess their impact on factors such as stability, deformation, and stress distribution. This iterative process allows us to refine designs, improve efficiency, and minimize costs while ensuring the desired level of performance and safety.

Our team's expertise, attention to detail, and commitment to delivering innovative and optimized mine designs sets us apart in the mining industry.

Performance Evaluation: Numerical modelling is valuable for evaluating the performance of geotechnical and mining systems over time. By incorporating data from monitoring systems or field measurements, we can calibrate and validate the models, ensuring their accuracy. This iterative process of model updating enhances the reliability of predictions and allows for ongoing assessment of system behavior throughout the project lifecycle.

Client Satisfaction: Our commitment to efficient 24/7 support is driven by a focus on client satisfaction. We measure our success by our clients' satisfaction with our support services. We continuously seek feedback to improve our support processes and ensure that we meet and exceed our clients' expectations.
